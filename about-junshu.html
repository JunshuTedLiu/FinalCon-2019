About Junshu

FinalCon 2019 Presenter: Junshu Liu

Junshu Liu, also known as Ted, is currently a master student at the University of Chicago majoring in Digital Studies of Language, Culture, and History. Since August 2014, he has been started his adventure, living, learning and discovering in the United States.

He is a developer, designer, and creator. Besides his concentrations regarding Digital Media, HCI, and UI/UX design, he has been studying data science for the researches of humanities, history, and culture over the past nine months. Related skills include data publication on the web, text analysis with NLP, data management and visualization using Python and R. You will see what he has been working on during the FinalCon 2019.

Learn more about Junshu by visiting his personal website, junshuliu.com.
